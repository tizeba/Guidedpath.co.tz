Guided Path is a registered NGO under the United Republic of Tanzania dedicated to promoting the right to mental health and wellness and Sustainable Development Goal No.3 on good health and wellbeing. As our name suggests, we seek to provide a guided path that will help individuals become the most empowered versions of themselves. We work with different groups including children, youth and marginalized groups in society. We strive to raise mental health awareness and wellness through innovative events, workshops, trainings, counseling sessions and life coaching that will shed more light on numerous mental health themes. At Guided Path we ardently believe that there is no health, without mental health.

Our mission

Our mission is to create a Tanzanian society that fosters a multi-faceted approach to mental wellness in which anyone, anywhere, can access the mental health resources and help they need. Guided Path envisions a Tanzanian community that is more conscious; as a result more open about addressing mental health related challenges within the society.

Our Vision

Guided Path envisions a community that is conscious about the importance of taking care of one’s mental health and where mental health services and resources are easily accessible to all.
